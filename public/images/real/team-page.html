Cantine Populaire is a friendly place to enjoy a healthy lunch!

Augustin and Emilie, brother and sister, created Cantine Populaire in 2018 with the desire to pass on their Love of healthy, gourmet family cooking! Everything is homemade with ultra-fresh, seasonal and mostly farm-grown, local produce and lots of love!!!! Monday to Friday for lunch 12:00-14:30, it's the ideal place for a quick, comfortable meal. At weekends, we offer a catering service and privatize our establishment.
